About Dead Ahead

Dead Ahead is the next evolution in the DEAD OverHead universe — a fully realized third-person action experience currently in active development by Iron Will Interactive.

Where DEAD OverHead forged its identity through gritty top-down combat and hyper-realistic UE5 visuals, Dead Ahead expands that DNA into a more cinematic dimension — deeper immersion, expanded combat mechanics, and a world you can truly step into.

The intense arcade combat loop you know is still the core. But this time, you're inside it.

With this new perspective comes a wave of new content, new threats, and major additions to the universe. Expect big things — the DEAD OverHead / Dead Ahead series is only getting started.

Where It Began

DEAD OverHead

DEAD OverHead is the game that started it all — a gritty, fast-paced top-down shooter built in Unreal Engine 5. Combining hyper-realistic visuals with responsive arcade-style combat, it set the foundation of what Iron Will Interactive could create.

Players pushed into brutal engagements against ChronoSoldiers — enemies wielding advanced technology from a distant timeline. Firefights, waves, and desperation. No mercy from the machine.

Dead Ahead takes everything DEAD OverHead built and expands it — new perspective, new mechanics, new scale. The universe doesn't stop here. There's much more to come for both titles and the world they share.

DEAD OverHead

DEAD OverHead is a gritty, fast-paced top-down shooter developed in Unreal Engine 5. The game combines hyper-realistic visuals with responsive arcade-style combat to deliver an experience that rewards both tactical thinking and reflexes.

Players face off against ChronoSoldiers — enemies equipped with advanced technology from a distant timeline. From combat drones and automated turrets to the undead and heavily armed infantry, no two engagements are the same.

Dead Ahead builds on this foundation — transitioning the battlefield from the overhead perspective into a fully immersive third-person combat experience. New perspective, expanded universe, more to come.
